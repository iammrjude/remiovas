# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful,
harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Give feedback with empathy and clarity.
- Assume good intent and ask questions before judging.
- Focus on what is best for the community and product.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or deliberate disruption.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly dismissing or belittling other contributors.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that violate
this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer
through the private contact method listed on the repository profile.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with care.
